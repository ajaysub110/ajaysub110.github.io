Biography

I am a final year undergraduate student at BITS Pilani, India and a core member at the Society for Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning. I am currently a research intern at Harvard University and MIT, working with Prof. Samuel Gershman on model-based reinforcement learning inspired by theories of human cognition. My primary interests are in using deep learning as a computational model for human perception and cognition. I believe that research in this direction can enhance our understanding of both human intelligence as well as help us develop better AI systems.
